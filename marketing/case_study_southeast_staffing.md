## The Problem

A regional staffing agency wanted to grow beyond inbound referrals — specifically into Manufacturing, where labor demand was high but their inroads were limited.

Their prospecting workflow before SCM Prospector:

- 🔍 Hand-search Indeed for companies actively posting vacancies → 15-20 minutes per company
- 📞 Cold-call gateway phone numbers → low connect rate, low ROI
- 💼 LinkedIn searches for HR contacts → time-consuming, inconsistent results
- ❌ No systematic way to filter out competitor staffing agencies from prospect lists
- 🎯 Repeatedly landing at the **C-suite** when looking for decision-makers — the wrong contact for a staffing services pitch

**Effective cost per qualified prospect**: 2-3 hours of recruiter time.

---

## The Configuration

SCM Prospector was set up specifically for their use case:

- **Manufacturing industry preset** — 15 pre-curated job-title keywords
- **Regional coverage** — Jacksonville, Gainesville, Daytona metro
- **Operational role priority** — HR Coordinator, Plant Manager, Talent Acquisition Specialist (instead of CEO/VP, which are the wrong target for staffing pitches)
- **Auto-filter staffing competitors** — Adecco, Robert Half, PrideStaff, and 20+ others excluded from results
- **Volume threshold** — minimum 5 vacancies posted in last 30 days (signal of active hiring urgency)
